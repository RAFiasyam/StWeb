IDN Boarding School

The IDN Middle School and Vocational Schools are under the IDN Foundation with the supervisor Mr. Dedi Gunawan, MT, CCIE. IDN Middle Schools and Vocational High Schools have a major advantage in the field of IT (information technology) which refers to the needs of the International IT industry. every child especially for the SMK level will be prepared to have skills and certifications to take part in the international world.

IDN Middle and Vocational Schools are supported by IDN/ID-Network and Imastudio which have many clients in Asia, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East so that with this experience we are trying to get down to the world of education to be able to attract more Indonesians so they can thrive in the international world.
